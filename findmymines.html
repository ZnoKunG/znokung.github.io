Anachronic
2D Puzzle Game

Game Details

Welcome to Anachronic, the 2D puzzle platformer where you'll journey through mystical ruins with an unsteady time flow. You play as a brave young girl who sets out to investigate the ruins of a collapsed laboratory.
Explore the ruins, twist time, and break through all obstacles in the vast underground landmarks and dark gothic atmospheres.
